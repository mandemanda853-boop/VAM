# VAMMalawiApp — Feature Overview (Categorized)

Fast Agent Digital Marketplace — a lightweight, local-first marketplace app for Malawi.

This document groups the app's features and policies into clear categories so website visitors can quickly find the information they're looking for.

---

## 1) Quick elevator pitch

VAMMalawiApp is a simple, fast marketplace that helps people buy, sell, and connect with neighbours across Malawi. Users can create listings with photos or short videos, surface items by location, and communicate securely — all from a mobile device.

## 2) Core features

- Home feed: shows recent listings, automatically sorted and updated as new posts arrive.
- Listings: create posts with photos or short video, description, price, and optional contact information.
- Save & prefer areas: save preferred locations and enable a preferred-area filter so your Home feed shows posts from the places you care about.
- Search & filters: a powerful search box with category and location hints for fast discovery. Supports keyword + location filtering and advanced queries.
- Messaging: built-in chat allows buyers and sellers to communicate directly; phone numbers are shown only if a seller chooses to publish them.
- Favorites: save posts for later and manage them in your profile.

## 3) Posting categories (examples)

You can post items across many categories. Common categories include:

- 🏠 Houses (rent or sale)
- 🏡 Plots & land
- 🚗 Vehicles (cars, motorbikes, etc.)
- 👗 Fashion & clothing
- 💻 Computers & accessories
- 🧩 Computer parts (RAM, storage, drives, etc.)
- ⚙️ Services & jobs
- 📱 Phones & electronics
- 🛠️ Home & garden
- And many more — categories are flexible to match local needs.

## 4) Search, discovery & feeds

- The Home feed prioritizes recent, relevant posts and respects preferred-area filters.
- Use category and location hints while searching to narrow results quickly.
- Advanced search supports combined keyword + location queries to find specific items or offers.

## 5) Accounts, sign-up & user actions

- Account creation: create a free account using email and phone. The in-app prompt guides new users through sign-up.
- After signing up you can publish posts, save favorites, and message other users.
- Manage your profile and saved areas from the account settings.

## 6) Data handling & privacy

- Minimal data: the app stores only the information necessary to operate (profile, posts, saved preferences, messages).
- Consent: sensitive fields such as phone numbers are published only with the user's explicit consent.
- Requests: users can request export or deletion of their data by contacting support.
- Data sharing: VAMMalawiApp does not sell personal data. It may share necessary data with trusted service providers (hosting, storage, email, analytics) and will disclose information to authorities only in response to lawful requests.

## 7) Retention & safety policies

- Automatic retention: messages and old posts may be removed after a period of inactivity to keep the marketplace current. Example: messages could be cleared after six months of inactivity (actual retention windows are determined by the app's policies).
- Inactive accounts or posts may be scheduled for deletion according to retention rules.
- Safety: guidelines and moderation policies help keep the marketplace safe and relevant for everyone.

## 8) Why we do this

We remove stale content and enforce retention to make it easier to find available items and connect with active users. That keeps the marketplace dynamic, relevant, and useful for both buyers and sellers.

## 9) Getting started (quick)

1. Download the app and create a free account using your email and phone number.
2. Complete your profile, add saved areas if you want curated feeds, then publish your first post.
3. Use the search box or Home feed to discover items; message sellers directly via chat.

## 10) Contact & support

For data requests, support, or to report issues, contact the support team from inside the app or via the website support page.
