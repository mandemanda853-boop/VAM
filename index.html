What VAMMalawiApp does

Fast Agent Digital Marketplace — a local-first app that helps people buy, sell and connect across Malawi.

Quick pitch

VAMMalawiApp makes it easy to create listings with photos or short videos, surface items by location, and communicate securely — all from your phone.

Core features

Home feed

Shows recent listings, automatically updated so you see fresh items from your preferred areas.

Search & filters

Powerful keyword and location search with category hints to help you find items quickly.

Messaging

Built-in chat for direct buyer-seller communication. Phone numbers are shown only with seller consent.

Favorites & saved areas

Save posts for later and define preferred areas to curate your Home feed.

Posting categories (examples)

  • 🏠 Houses (rent / sale)
  • 🏡 Plots & land
  • 🚗 Vehicles
  • 👗 Fashion & clothing
  • 💻 Computers & accessories
  • 📱 Phones & electronics
  • ⚙️ Services & jobs
  • 🛠️ Home & garden

Privacy & data

The app stores only the information necessary to operate. Sensitive fields (like phone numbers) are published only with explicit consent. Users can request data export or deletion via support.

Retention & safety

To keep the marketplace relevant we remove stale content over time. Messages and inactive posts may be cleared according to retention rules (for example, messages may be removed after six months of inactivity).

Getting started

  1. Download the app and create a free account with email and phone.
  2. Complete your profile, add saved areas, and publish your first post.
  3. Use the Home feed or search to find items and message sellers directly.

Contact & support

For help, data requests, or to report issues, contact support from inside the app or via the website support page. For updates visit our Facebook page: VAMMalawiapp.
